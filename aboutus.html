About Our Civic Infrastructure Platform

Civic infrastructure plays a crucial role in ensuring a **smooth and functional society**. Unfortunately, many issues go **unresolved** due to **inefficient reporting systems and a lack of tracking mechanisms**.

This platform helps citizens **report issues, track complaints, and receive timely updates** on their concerns. It serves as a bridge between the community and government services, making sure civic problems are addressed efficiently.

What Problems Can You Report?

  • Electricity shortages, faulty transformers, and streetlight failures.
  • Potholes, broken roads, and poor transportation infrastructure.
  • Water supply issues, sewage leaks, and sanitation concerns.
  • Garbage collection delays and improper waste management.

Our Goal

We aim to create a **faster and more accountable** civic complaint system to ensure that infrastructure problems do not disrupt daily life. **Your participation helps improve the city for everyone!**
